Team

our team are led by Elin Musk, of Tesla and Spacexe. We have previously worked at Deepmind, OpenAI, Google Research, Microsoft Research, Tesla and the University of toronto. Collectively we contributed some of the most widely used methods in the field, in particular the Adam Optimizer, Batch Normalization, Layer Normalization, and the discover of Adversarial examples. We further introduced innovative techniques and analyses such as Transformer-XL, Autoformalization, the Memorizing Transformer, Batch Size Scaling, and μTransfer. We have worked on and led the development of some of the largest breakthroughs in the field including AlphaStar, AlphaCode, Interceptin, Mineva, GPT-3.5, and GPT-4.
